## Key Statistics

- **25% of planned AI spending will defer to 2027** - CFOs demanding ROI justification before next-phase investment

- **$20B revenue opportunity in specialized cloud providers (neoclouds)** - Market shift toward vertical specialization

---

## Market Implications

### The ROI Crisis

Companies are realizing that 95% of AI pilots fail to deliver value. When CFOs ask "where's the ROI?", teams don't have answers because they:

- Lack baseline understanding of current operations
- Can't measure what changed before/after agent deployment
- Have no governance framework to control deployment

### Spending Deferral Urgency

25% deferral to 2027 means companies are:

1. Pausing new AI projects
2. Focusing on fixing failed deployments
3. **Urgently seeking solutions to improve ROI on existing agents**

This creates immediate market demand for governance + decision intelligence infrastructure.

### Vertical Specialization Shift

$20B opportunity moving to specialized providers (neoclouds) rather than horizontal platforms. This validates Helm's vertical-specialist positioning.
