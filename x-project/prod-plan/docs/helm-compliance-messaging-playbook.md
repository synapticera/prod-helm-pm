## Path 1: Aggressive Compliance Focus

### Target: Chief Compliance Officer / Legal Department

**Subject Line Options**:

- "EU AI Act Deadline: August 2, 2026 → Are You Ready?"
- "€30M Fine Risk: Is Your AI Agent Governance Compliant?"
- "3 Actions Before August 2, 2026 to Avoid AI Compliance Penalties"

**Email Opening**:

```
Dear [Title],

The EU AI Act compliance deadline is 7 months away (August 2, 2026).

If your organization deploys AI agents in customer service, you need to demonstrate:
✓ Complete decision transparency (why did the agent decide?)
✓ Human oversight mechanisms (how can humans override?)
✓ Risk management documentation (what risks were identified?)
✓ Post-market monitoring (are agents performing as intended?)

Organizations that can't prove compliance face:
- Fines up to €30M or 6% of global turnover (whichever is higher)
- System shutdowns until compliance is demonstrated
- Regulatory enforcement and reputational damage

Helm is the infrastructure that makes you compliant by August 2.
```

**Landing Page Headline**:

```
Helm: The Infrastructure for EU AI Act Compliance
Audit trails. Decision transparency. Human oversight.
Ready by August 2, 2026.
```

**Key Value Props** (Compliance Focus):

1. **Decision Lineage**: Every agent decision is documented with context, policies, and reasoning
2. **Audit Trail**: Complete record for Notified Body certification review
3. **Human Oversight**: Enforcement of review gates for decisions exceeding confidence thresholds
4. **Risk Documentation**: Automatic capture of decision patterns for risk assessment
5. **Compliance Dashboard**: Real-time evidence that system meets regulatory requirements

**Sales Demo Flow**:

1. Show current compliance gap (demo without Helm)
2. Show Helm audit trail (demo with Helm)
3. Show Notified Body readiness report (generated automatically)
4. Discuss implementation timeline (3 months to compliance)

**Messaging by Stakeholder**:

**For CFO**: "The cost of non-compliance (€30M fine) vastly exceeds the cost of Helm. Compliance is mandatory."

**For CIO**: "Helm provides the infrastructure your legal team needs to certify compliance. No custom engineering required."

**For General Counsel**: "Helm generates the documentation third-party Notified Bodies require for compliance certification."
