## Key Statistics by Category

### Market Inflection (Why Now)

- **40%** of enterprise apps will have AI agents by end 2026 (Gartner)
- **23%** currently scaling agentic systems (McKinsey)
- **39%** experimenting with agents (McKinsey)
- **33%** of service AI deployments will fail (CustomerThink/Synaptic)

### Financial Impact (Why It Matters)

- **$120M** additional profit per $500M company at Stage 2→3 transition (MIT)
- **+24pp** profitability swing from Stage 2 to Stage 3 (MIT)
- **61%** not seeing enterprise-level EBIT impact despite deployment (McKinsey)
- **+3x** better outcomes with systematic approach vs ad-hoc (Helm analysis)

### Governance & Complexity (Why Helm Wins)

- **2x** more risks managed in 2025 vs 2022 (McKinsey)
- **60%** of Fortune 100 appointing Chief AI Officer (Synaptic)
- **5-10%** of tech budgets allocated to agentic AI for 3-5 years (Bain)
- **Workflow redesign** = #1 factor in EBIT impact (McKinsey)

### Market Opportunity (TAM)

- **34%** of enterprises in pilot phase (Stage 2) = direct addressable market (MIT)
- **61%** seeing only use-case benefits, not enterprise ROI = pain point (McKinsey)
- **5-10%** annual tech spend on agentic AI (3-5 years) = consistent budget (Bain)
- **$1T+** productivity opportunity across enterprise functions

### Timing (Urgency)

- **August 2, 2026**: EU AI Act fully applicable (mandatory governance)
- **Q2-Q3 2026**: CFO ROI reviews begin (25% spending deferral per Forrester)
- **2026**: Market inflection year (agent adoption moving mainstream)
- **12-18 months**: Window for first-mover category advantage

1. **Open with impact** (2 min)

3. **Prove EBIT gap** (2 min)
   - Reference: [McKinsey Report](../2026-01-05-McKinsey-State-of-AI-2025-Summary.md)
   - Explain: "88% deployment, 39% ROI = workflow redesign problem"

4. **Validate with convergence** (2 min)
   - Reference: [Synthesis Document](../2026-01-05-Research-Synthesis-MIT-McKinsey-Bain.md)
   - Show: MIT, McKinsey, Bain all validate same insights

5. **Strategic positioning** (2 min)
   - Reference: [Helm Strategy Master](../helm-strategy-master.md)
   - Explain: Why domain specialization + governance first = wins

### Service Leader Pitch Sequence

1. **Show the problem** (2 min)
   - Reference: [Research Master](../helm-research-master.md) - "33% of AI service deployments fail"
   - Relate: "This might be happening in your organization"

2. **Explain the root cause** (2 min)
   - Reference: [McKinsey Report](../2026-01-05-McKinsey-State-of-AI-2025-Summary.md)
   - Show: Workflow redesign is #1 success factor (not just deployment)

3. **Prove the impact** (2 min)
   - Reference: [MIT Report](../2026-01-05-MIT-CISR-Enterprise-AI-Maturity-Model-Summary.md)
   - Explain: Companies that do this see 5-7x better outcomes

4. **Offer the solution** (2 min)
   - Reference: [Helm Strategy Master](../helm-strategy-master.md) - Understand → Plan → Execute → Monitor lifecycle
   - Show: How Helm enables each stage

5. **Create urgency** (1 min)

   - Mention: "EU AI Act deadline August 2, 2026"
