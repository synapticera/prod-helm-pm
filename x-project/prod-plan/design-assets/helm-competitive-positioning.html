Strategic Insight

Helm's Unique Position: We own the intersection of high decision intelligence depth AND low customer acquisition cost. Legacy vendors (Celonis, UiPath) have deep capabilities but are trapped in enterprise-only, $100K+ deal models. Incumbent platforms (Salesforce, Microsoft) have scale and existing relationships but lack decision discovery capabilities. Helm serves the 150,000 SMBs and mid-market companies that incumbent platforms ignore, while offering deeper decision intelligence than generic process mining tools.

Helm's Defensibility Moat

  • Network Effects: Decision data from every customer improves recommendations for all users (fleet learning). More customers = smarter product = stronger moat
  • Category Ownership: First to claim "Decision Intelligence for Customer Service" before competitors catch up. Analyst coverage and brand equity compound
  • Vertical Specialization: Deep domain expertise in customer service that horizontal platforms can't match. Defensible versus generalists
  • Regulatory Tailwind: EU AI Act compliance built in. Harder for others to retrofit governance to existing systems
  • Product-Led Growth: Freemium model creates switching costs. Millions of free users generate data, insights, and expansion opportunities competitors can't replicate
