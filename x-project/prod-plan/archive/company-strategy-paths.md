#strategy paths

context: - services business - $4m salesforce consulting - AI focused - moderate IP enablement - moderate business model innovation - ok team - product business - promising AI play (aka HELM) - early

1. Lean
   services: lean, cash flow positive, contributes to product

   product: lean, consumes cash but modest

   operations: lean, automated

   pros: - focus
   cons: - only one chip on the wheel - team moral

   questions: - how much cash we generate from svcs? - team moral

2. Liquidate services
   services: sell for $5m, get focus, use cash to fund product

   product: invest with proceeds, more aggressive

   operations: lean, automated

   pros: - product focus and capitalization

   cons: - only one chip on wheel - transactions are hard and will take a lot of focus

   questions: - can you get cash up front? - do we have a blue bird with RS?

3. Growth both
   services: hire new leader and provide working capital to grow as an AI specialist

   product: raise more VC to get after it

   operations: lean, automated

   pros:
   opportunity to make money on both

   cons:
   challenge of running 2 businesses and focus

   questions: - how much cash for the services business - interest in putting any money into the services business
