Helm Problem Slide — Concept Exploration

Four alternative approaches to the problem statement slide

Critique of Original Slide

Copy Issues

  • "AI tsunami" is cliché and overused
  • "bottleneck to progress" is passive—no urgency
  • Four gaps are abstract, don't hit emotionally
  • "ground truth" is insider jargon
  • Conclusion "new platform" is weak—doesn't name Helm
  • No data or proof points to establish credibility

Visual Issues

  • Text-heavy, reads like bullet points not a presentation
  • No visual hierarchy beyond color differences
  • No emotional impact or storytelling
  • Missing the powerful McKinsey data (88% vs 39%)
  • Generic circles as bullets—no visual interest
  • No visual metaphor to anchor the message
Concept 1
The ROI Gap
Data-driven approach showing the 88% vs 39% gap visually with bar charts and a focused "Why the Gap Exists" panel.
Strengths
Uses credible McKinsey data. Clear visual gap. Concrete "why" explanation. Professional dark theme.
