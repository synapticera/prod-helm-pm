# Staircase AI Research

**Date:** January 10, 2026
**Category:** Competitive Analysis - Customer Intelligence Platform
**Status:** Acquired by Gainsight

---

## Overview

Staircase AI is a **Customer Relationship Intelligence** platform that uses AI to analyze millions of customer interactions and turn them into actionable insights. Acquired by Gainsight, it's positioned as the entry point for companies not yet ready for full Customer Success Platforms.

**Tagline:** "Customer Intelligence and Analytics Platform"

---

## Key Capabilities

### 1. AI-Powered Health Scores

- Objectively measures customer sentiment, real-time risks, stakeholder changes
- 86.2% more accurate than rule-based health scoring systems
- Auto-updates based on communication signals

### 2. Churn Prediction

- AI churn prediction scores™ based on historical account data
- 95% accuracy in renewal forecasting
- Early warning signals for at-risk customers

### 3. Communication Analysis

- Analyzes emails, tickets, Slack messages, calls, meetings
- 2.2M+ monthly communications processed
- 90%+ accuracy in sentiment detection
- Zero manual data entry required

### 4. Stakeholder Intelligence

- Maps stakeholders and identifies new contacts
- Alerts on engagement changes (someone leaves, transitions, goes underwater)
- Tracks "Decision Maker" and "Influencer" roles
- Relationship heatmaps using network analysis

### 5. AI Summaries

- Automated analysis of accounts, calls, meetings
- Highlights lowlights, highlights, issues, action items
- "Ask Staircase" natural language query feature
