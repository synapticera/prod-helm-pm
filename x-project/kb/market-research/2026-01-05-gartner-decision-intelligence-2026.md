## What Is Decision Intelligence?

**Gartner Definition**: "Decision Intelligence is the engineering discipline of how decisions are made"

### Core Components

- Combines LLMs (probabilistic reasoning) with knowledge graphs + rules (symbolic reasoning)
- Enables "Composite AI" that's both intelligent AND explainable
- Provides governance layer for safe, scalable agent deployment

---

## Why #1 Trend?

### Market Drivers

1. **Agent Explosion** - 40% of enterprise apps deploying agents by end 2026
2. **Governance Gap** - Companies realize agents can't self-regulate
3. **Compliance Pressure** - EU AI Act forces mandatory decision transparency
4. **ROI Crisis** - Need to understand decisions to measure value

### What Companies Need

- Visibility into how agents are making decisions
- Ability to course-correct when agents go wrong
- Audit trails for compliance and transparency
- Continuous learning from decision outcomes

## How It Applies to Helm

### Helm as Decision Intelligence Platform

- **Mine Module** = Captures decision traces (what is being decided, by whom, why)
- **Design Module** = Uses decision data to design better processes
- **Manage Module** = Enforces governance guardrails on agent decisions
- **Network Effects** = Fleet learning makes collective decisions smarter

### Technical Validation

Gartner's "Decision Intelligence" framework validates Helm's entire approach:

1. Make decisions visible (Mine)
2. Design with data (Design)
