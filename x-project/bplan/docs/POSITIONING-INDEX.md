## Key Concepts Index

### The Executive Function Lifecycle
**Definition**: Five-stage framework for successful agent deployment
1. **UNDERSTAND** (Mine): What decisions are we making today?
2. **PLAN** (Design): What should agents decide vs. what stays human?
3. **SELL** (Design transparency): Who's bought in? Who has concerns?
4. **EXECUTE** (Manage): Are agents following the plan? Is every decision auditable?
5. **MONITOR & CORRECT** (Monitor): Are agents delivering intended outcomes?

**Why It Matters**: 95% of AI projects fail because companies skip stages 1-3 and jump to execution. Companies executing the full lifecycle see 5-7x better outcomes.

**Materials**: [Lifecycle One-Pager](helm-lifecycle-one-pager.html) | [Messaging Guide](helm-messaging-guide.md) | [Product Positioning](helm-product-positioning.md)

---

### Decision Intelligence
**Definition**: Infrastructure that captures "why" decisions are made, not just that they were made
- Decision Traces: Complete records with context and lineage
- Context Graphs: Structured data showing what matters
- Decision Policies: Explicit specifications agents follow
- Precedent Libraries: Learning systems that improve over time

**Why It Matters**: Compliance audits, continuous improvement, defensible moat from decision data.

**Materials**: [Product Positioning - Decision Intelligence Section](helm-product-positioning.md#what-is-decision-intelligence) | [Positioning Document - Category Section](helm-positioning.md#the-emerging-category)

---

### Constraint-Hunting Principle
**Definition**: Attack the NEXT constraint before it becomes bottleneck, not the current one

**Pattern**:
- Current constraint gets solved â†’ becomes commoditized
- Winners attack next constraint â†’ defensible positioning
- Example: Email (current = send messages, commoditized; next = organize/search, Gmail won)

**Why It Matters**: Explains why Helm wins (we're solving management, not agents).

**Materials**: [Messaging Guide - Constraint Hunting](helm-messaging-guide.md#the-constraint-hunting-principle) | [Positioning Document](helm-positioning.md#the-strategic-principle-attack-the-next-constraint-not-the-current-one)
