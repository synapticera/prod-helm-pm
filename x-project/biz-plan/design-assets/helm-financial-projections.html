Key Assumptions & Notes

  • Free-to-Paid Conversion: Year 1 starts at 2% (50K→1K paying), increases to 30–40% at scale (2M→600K+ paying) through network effects and proven ROI
  • Growth Drivers: Freemium viral coefficient 0.6+, Explore→Mine 60% conversion, Mine→Design 40% conversion, Design→Manage 50% conversion
  • Gross Margin: 80–85% from day 1 (software product, minimal COGS). Supports sales/marketing expansion without burn acceleration
  • Path to Profitability: EBITDA positive Year 2–3 when growth moderates. Operationally positive Year 3–4. Sustainable cash generation by Year 5
  • Magic Number: Revenue growth efficiency target 0.75–1.0 by Year 2+. Rule of 40 target (growth % + FCF margin ≥ 40%)
  • Market Expansion: Phase 1 (Y1–2) SMB domination. Phase 2 (Y2–3) Mid-Market. Phase 3 (Y3+) Enterprise & vertical expansion (Finance, IT, HR)
