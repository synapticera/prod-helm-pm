## 1. High-Level Market Statistics

### ROI Metrics (Industry-Wide)

- **Average ROI**: $3.50 for every $1 invested in AI customer service
- **Top performers**: Up to 8x ROI
- **Cost per interaction**: AI $0.50 vs Human $6.00 (12x cheaper)
- **Hybrid model results**: 30% cost reduction, 40% productivity gains, 25% CSAT improvement
- **Resolution rate improvement**: 65% of queries resolved without human intervention (up from 52% in 2023)

### Market Size

- AI for customer service market: $12.06B (2024) → $47.82B (2030)
- CAGR: 25.8%
