## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric              | Before   | After     | Change         |
| ------------------- | -------- | --------- | -------------- |
| Response Time       | Baseline | 5x faster | 5x improvement |
| Inquiry Volume      | 100%     | 50%       | -50% reduction |
| Channel Mix (Voice) | 80%      | 50%       | -37.5% shift   |
| Channel Mix (Chat)  | 20%      | 50%       | +150% shift    |

### Qualitative Results

- 5x reduction in response times through automation
- 50% reduction in overall inquiry volume
- Shifted from 80% voice-heavy model to balanced 50/50 voice-chat split
- More efficient channel mix reducing operational costs
- AI handles routine banking queries autonomously
- Canadian fintech scaling support without proportional headcount
