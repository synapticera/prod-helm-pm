## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                | Before               | After              | Change        |
| --------------------- | -------------------- | ------------------ | ------------- |
| Markets Covered       | Individual solutions | 32 markets unified | Single bot    |
| Departments Supported | Limited              | 50 departments     | Full coverage |
| Languages Supported   | 1-2                  | 5 languages        | 5 languages   |
| Human Agent Handover  | Slow                 | Instantaneous      | Seamless      |

### Qualitative Results

- Single AI bot fronting 50 different departments across 32 markets
- Conversational support in 5 languages: Haitian Creole, Spanish, French, Dutch, English
- Instantaneous handover to live agents when needed
- Unified customer experience across Caribbean and Central American markets
- Telecom-specific queries handled autonomously
- Scaled regional operations without market-by-market teams
