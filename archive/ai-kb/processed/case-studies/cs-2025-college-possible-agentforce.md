## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                | Before          | After                 | Change          |
| --------------------- | --------------- | --------------------- | --------------- |
| Student Capacity      | 6,250 per coach | 25,000 per coach      | 4x increase     |
| Total Students Served | ~6,250          | 25,000 per year       | 4x capacity     |
| Coach Research Time   | High            | Significantly reduced | Major reduction |

### Qualitative Results

- Agentforce empowers College Possible to serve 4x as many students with the same number of coaches
- Coaches spend less time researching and more time directly interacting with students
- 25,000 students served annually through AI-augmented coaching
- Research and administrative tasks automated, allowing human touch where it matters most
- Scaled impact without proportional headcount growth
