### Quantitative Results

| ----------------------- | -------- | ---------- | -------------------- |

### Qualitative Results

- Publisher and trusted leader in research and learning
- Service call spikes during back-to-school season
- Thousands of students use Wiley educational resources yearly
- Agentforce answers questions using knowledge base
- Automatically resolves account access issues and password resets
- Triages registration and payment issues
- Directs students to appropriate resources
- Einstein helps reps draft responses grounded in business context
- Case summaries help supervisors get caught up quicker
- Summaries used to spot trends and provide product feedback
- "Piloting Agentforce has made a noticeable difference during one of our busiest periods"
