The Agentic Shift

A comprehensive index and analysis of AI in Customer Service (2025-2027). Exploring the transition from scripted chatbots to autonomous agents, backed by case studies from Klarna, Morgan Stanley, and global leaders.

The Evolution: From Scripted to Autonomous

We are witnessing a fundamental change in customer service technology. Traditional "Gen 1" chatbots relied on rigid decision trees and keywords, often frustrating users. The new "Gen 2" Agentic AI utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to reason, plan, and execute complex workflows autonomously. This shift isn't just about answering FAQs—it's about resolving issues.

Case Study: Morgan Stanley

Strategy: Expert Augmentation

In contrast to Klarna, Morgan Stanley built "AskResearchGPT" to empower, not replace, their financial advisors. The goal was to synthesize vast amounts of data to improve the quality of human advice.
