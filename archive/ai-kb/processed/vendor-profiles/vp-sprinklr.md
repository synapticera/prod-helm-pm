## Limitations

1. **Complexity**: Full platform may be overwhelming for simpler needs
2. **Cost**: Enterprise pricing can be significant
3. **Social Focus**: Core strength in social/digital may not suit voice-heavy centers
4. **Implementation Time**: Full deployment can take months
