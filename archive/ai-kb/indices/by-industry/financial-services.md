## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **Proprietary Solutions at Scale**: Largest banks (BofA, Capital One, JPM) building custom AI assistants
- **Agent Augmentation Focus**: Verint leading agent-assist implementations (Fiserv 96% coverage)
- **Fintech Speed**: Digital-first players achieving results in weeks (Sharesies 12 weeks)
- **Voice AI Maturity**: HSBC, Kiwibank showing 98%+ intent handling

### Regulatory Considerations

- PCI DSS compliance for card data handling
- KYC/AML requirements for customer verification
- Fair lending and bias prevention in AI decisions
- CFPB consumer protection requirements
- Audit trail and explainability mandates

### Common Use Cases

1. Account inquiries and balance checks
2. Transaction disputes and fraud alerts
3. Payment processing and transfers
4. Loan/credit application status
5. Investment portfolio questions
6. Card activation and security

### ROI Drivers

- High call volume in consumer banking
- Compliance-heavy workflows benefiting from automation
- 24/7 global customer expectations
- Fraud prevention savings
- Agent quality and compliance monitoring
