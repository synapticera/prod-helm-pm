## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **Disruption Management**: AI handling rebooking during weather/operational disruptions
- **Multi-Language Scale**: Airlines deploying across 30+ markets (IndiGo 300+ journeys)
- **Agentforce Adoption**: Hotels (Wyndham, Safari365) using Salesforce for integrated guest experience
- **Voice AI Maturity**: Amtrak's Julie handling 5M+ questions annually

### Seasonal Considerations

- Peak travel seasons require 3x capacity scaling
- Holiday booking surges need instant response
- Weather disruption events create volume spikes
- Loyalty program inquiries spike around promotions

### Common Use Cases

1. Flight/booking status and changes
2. Trip planning and recommendations
3. Loyalty program inquiries
4. Disruption rebooking and compensation
5. Ancillary sales (upgrades, extras)
6. Check-in and boarding assistance

### ROI Drivers

- High call volume during disruptions
- 24/7 global traveler expectations
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities
- Peak season staffing challenges
- Multilingual support requirements
