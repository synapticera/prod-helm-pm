## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **In-Store AI**: Target, Home Depot, Lowe's deploying associate-facing AI tools
- **Revenue-Generating Chatbots**: Ada implementations driving direct sales (Simba Sleep £600K/month)
- **Inventory Operations**: Agentforce reducing inventory redistribution from 10 days to 1 hour
- **Global Scale**: Estée Lauder deploying across 25 brands and 150 countries

### Seasonal Considerations

- Black Friday/Cyber Monday volume spikes (5-10x normal)
- Holiday return season (January surge)
- Back-to-school shopping peaks
- Flash sale events creating instant demand

### Common Use Cases

1. Order status and tracking
2. Returns and exchanges
3. Product recommendations
4. Store/inventory availability
5. Loyalty program inquiries
6. Sizing and product questions
7. In-store associate assistance

### ROI Drivers

- High transaction volume with predictable questions
- Returns/exchanges automation potential
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities
- Seasonal staffing flexibility
- Omnichannel experience requirements
