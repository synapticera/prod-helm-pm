## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **WhatsApp Scheduling**: Brazilian hospitals achieving 82% booking increases through WhatsApp AI
- **Revenue Cycle Automation**: AKASA achieving 71% account automation with ~14 FTE savings
- **Clinical AI Safety**: Kaiser Permanente demonstrating 97.7% accuracy for urgent detection
- **Documentation Relief**: Advocate Health reducing physician documentation time by 50%+

### Provider vs Payer Distinction

| Aspect        | Provider              | Payer                   |
| ------------- | --------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Primary focus | Patient care delivery | Benefits administration |
| AI use cases  | Scheduling, clinical  | Member services, PA     |
| Key metric    | Patient access        | Resolution rate         |
| Revenue model | Fee-for-service/value | Premium/risk            |
| Regulatory    | HIPAA + clinical      | HIPAA + insurance       |

### Common Use Cases

1. Appointment scheduling and reminders
2. Patient intake and registration
3. Billing inquiries and payment
4. Prescription refill requests
5. Test results and follow-up
6. Care navigation
7. Prior authorization (provider-side)

### ROI Drivers

- High patient call volume
- No-show reduction through AI reminders
- Staff shortage challenges
- Revenue cycle acceleration
- Physician time savings
- Patient experience/HCAHPS scores

### Regulatory Considerations

- HIPAA privacy requirements
- Clinical accuracy and safety
- Medical device regulations (if applicable)
- State medical practice laws
- Value-based care quality metrics
