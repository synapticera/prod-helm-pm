## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **Agent Retention Focus**: DTE Energy achieving 94% reduction in agent attrition through AI support
- **Multi-Agent Scale**: E.ON deploying 30+ AI agents covering 70% of demand
- **Post-Call Automation**: Verint implementations saving 35 seconds per call in wrap-up
- **Form Elimination**: Duke Energy reducing forms by 90% through conversational AI

### Seasonal Considerations

- Summer/winter peak usage periods
- Storm season outage surges
- Billing cycle spikes
- Rate change announcements
- Energy assistance program enrollment periods

### Common Use Cases

1. Billing inquiries and payment
2. Outage reporting and status
3. Service start/stop/transfer
4. Energy efficiency programs
5. Budget billing enrollment
6. Payment arrangements
7. High bill investigations

### ROI Drivers

- High call volume with predictable patterns
- Outage surge capacity needs
- Agent turnover challenges
- Regulatory compliance requirements
- Customer retention in competitive markets
- Self-service potential for routine transactions

### Regulatory Considerations

- Public utility commission oversight
- Mandated response times during outages
- Payment arrangement requirements
- Disconnection notice regulations
- Low-income assistance programs
- Energy efficiency program reporting
