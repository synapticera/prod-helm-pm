## Strengths

1. **Voice AI Leadership**: Kiwibank case shows 98% voice intents resolved without live agent - strongest voice AI evidence among all vendors

2. **Highest Documented ROI**: Genesys Customer Zero shows 9.8x cumulative ROI over 3 years - exceptional long-term return

3. **Employee Experience Focus**: Virgin Atlantic case uniquely measures employee happiness (+12 points), demonstrating AI benefits to workforce

4. **Predictive Engagement**: IONOS case shows 68% sales conversion increase through AI timing optimization - unique among contact center vendors

5. **Enterprise Scale Proven**: All 6 case studies are large enterprises (600K+ customers, national banks, major airlines)

6. **Comprehensive Metrics**: Virgin Atlantic case covers handle time, CSAT, FCR, answer rates, and employee happiness in single deployment

7. **Rapid Voice Deployment**: Kiwibank deployed functional voicebot in 4 months, showing accelerated implementation

8. **Revenue Impact**: IONOS shows 29% revenue per customer visit increase - rare direct revenue attribution

## Limitations

1. **Enterprise-Only Focus**: All case studies are large enterprises; limited SMB/mid-market evidence

2. **Higher Complexity**: Full CCaaS platform may be over-engineered for simple use cases

3. **No Standalone AI Option**: AI capabilities tied to Genesys Cloud platform

4. **Resolution Rate Gap**: Case studies focus on efficiency metrics; less autonomous resolution rate data vs. Ada/Intercom

5. **Limited Regional Evidence**: Strong in APAC (Australia, NZ) but fewer North American case studies

6. **Private Company**: PE ownership means less visibility into long-term roadmap

## Competitive Position

### Best For

- **Voice-First Contact Centers**: Strongest voice AI evidence among all vendors
- **Large Enterprise Operations**: Proven at 600K+ customer scale
- **Revenue Optimization**: Predictive engagement shows sales impact
- **Employee Experience Focus**: Unique EX metrics (Virgin Atlantic +12 happiness)
- **Complex Multi-Channel Operations**: Full omnichannel platform

### Not Ideal For

- **SMB/Mid-Market**: Platform complexity and pricing may be excessive
- **Chat-First Operations**: Intercom/Zendesk may be simpler for digital-only
- **Salesforce-Centric Environments**: Agentforce provides tighter CRM integration
- **AI-Only Needs**: Full CCaaS may be overkill for AI-only requirements

### Key Competitors

- **NICE CXone** - Similar enterprise CCaaS; comparable AI capabilities
- **Five9** - Comparable cloud contact center; Salesforce partnership
- **Amazon Connect** - Lower cost; AWS ecosystem integration
- **Salesforce Service Cloud/Agentforce** - Deeper CRM integration
- **Verint** - Stronger in workforce management and analytics
