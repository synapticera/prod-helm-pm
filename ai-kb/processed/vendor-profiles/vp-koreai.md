## Best Fit Scenarios

**Ideal For:**

- Enterprise HR/employee service desks
- Banking/financial services with multilingual needs
- Organizations seeking CX + EX on one platform
- Companies needing strong agent assist capabilities
- GenAI-first deployments with guardrails

**Less Ideal For:**

- SMB deployments with limited budgets
- Simple single-use-case chatbots
- Organizations heavily invested in competing platforms
- Voice-only IVR modernization projects
