## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                  | Before   | After         | Improvement              |
| ----------------------- | -------- | ------------- | ------------------------ |
| Call handling cost      | 1x       | 0.33x         | 67% cost reduction       |
| Inquiry completion time | Variable | ~2 min        | ~2 minute completion     |
| Monthly call volume     | 1M+      | Handled by AI | 1M+ calls/month capacity |
| Sentence accuracy       | -        | 90-95%        | 90-95% accuracy          |
| Language models         | -        | 7             | 7 custom language models |
| Acoustic models         | -        | 2             | 2 custom acoustic models |

### Qualitative Results

- Faster, friendlier, more consistent service
- No need to speak with live agent for routine inquiries
- Pre-service eligibility verification
- Authorization and referral information
- Several pending patent submissions
- Eliminated long wait times for members
