## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                          | Before       | After      | Improvement        |
| ------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------- | ------------------ |
| Conversations handled (1 month) | Human agents | 2.3M by AI | -                  |
| Equivalent human agents         | 700 FTEs     | AI         | 700 FTE equivalent |
| Resolution time                 | 11 minutes   | 2 minutes  | 82% reduction      |
| Repeat inquiries                | Baseline     | -25%       | 25% reduction      |
| Chat volume handled by AI       | 0%           | 66%        | +66%               |

### Qualitative Results

- Customer satisfaction levels comparable to human agents
- Greater accuracy in issue resolution
- 24/7 availability across global markets

### ROI / Business Impact

- **ROI**: Estimated $40M annual savings (700 FTE equivalent)
- **Payback Period**: Immediate
- **Cost Savings**: ~$40M/year labor equivalent
