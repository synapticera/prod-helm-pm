## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric               | Before           | After      | Change           |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | ---------- | ---------------- |
| AI Resolution Rate   | 4% (old chatbot) | 44%        | 11x improvement  |
| Resolution Time      | 3 hours          | 23 minutes | 87% reduction    |
| CSAT Score           | 87%              | 92%        | +5 points        |
| Monthly Interactions | 30,000+          | 30,000+    | Scale maintained |

### Qualitative Results

- Appointment scheduling SaaS for beauty, wellness, and fitness businesses
- Previous simple chatbot only achieved 4% resolution rate
- Initially targeted 8-10% resolution, achieved 44% in 3 months
- AI enables team to "do more with less"
- Sets new standard for beauty and wellness industry support
- Empowers customers to self-service common inquiries
- Reduced demand on 80-person support team
