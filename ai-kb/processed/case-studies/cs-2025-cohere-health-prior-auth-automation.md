### Qualitative Results

- Works with 660,000+ providers
- Handles 12+ million prior authorization requests annually
- 9 million FHIR-based authorizations processed in past year
- AI auto-approves up to 90% of requests
- 47% reduction in administrative costs
- 85% of prior auths handled in real-time (exceeding 80% industry target)
- 61% reduction in provider data-entry time
- 50% faster medical necessity reviews with 99%+ accuracy
- 93% provider satisfaction across 600,000+ clinicians
- Cohere Align personalizes PA based on provider history
- 80% of submissions streamlined for pre-approved providers
- 55% cut in provider submission time
- 98% provider satisfaction for Align users
- Partnership with Microsoft Dragon Copilot for ambient PA at point of care
- Named to Inc. 5000 list and Gartner Hype Cycle for US Healthcare Payers
