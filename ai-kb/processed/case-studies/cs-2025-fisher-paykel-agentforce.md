### Qualitative Results

- Global leader in premium household appliances
- Operates in 50+ countries
- Strives to be most human-centered appliance brand
- Employees lacked visibility into customer interaction histories
- Agentforce reasons across 10,000+ knowledge articles
- Custom retriever optimized for semantic search
- Pinpoints exact appliance and potential issues from natural language
- Proactive notifications for appliance anomalies (e.g., freezer temperature)
- AI diagnoses problems, alerts service team, schedules appointments
- Passes work orders to technicians with job-specific details
- Many products are IoT-enabled with real-time performance updates
- Operators can service customers globally with same processes
