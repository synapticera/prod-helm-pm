### Qualitative Results

- Harvard Business School case study on AI journey
- BenefitsGPT allows queries of unstructured data (plan details, coverage, exclusions)
- Aggregates data across departments for benefit analysts
- Enables millions of plan-holders improved customer service
- Reduced call times for member inquiries
- ContractsGPT for contract analysis
- SecureGPT for secure AI interactions
- Formed industry consortium with other BCBS affiliates
- Comprehensive AI strategy focused on transforming work processes
- $600 million savings target over three years from AI tools
