## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                        | Before   | After   | Improvement            |
| ----------------------------- | -------- | ------- | ---------------------- |
| Lead response time            | Hours    | Minutes | Near-instant response  |
| Inquiry to viewing conversion | Baseline | +45%    | 45% higher conversion  |

### Qualitative Results

- AI chatbot provides immediate lead nurturing

- No missed opportunities due to after-hours inquiries
- Sales team focuses on closing rather than initial inquiries
- Improved customer satisfaction and engagement
