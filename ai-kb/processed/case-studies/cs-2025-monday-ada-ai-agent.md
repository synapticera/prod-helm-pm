## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric               | Before   | After    | Change               |
| -------------------- | -------- | -------- | -------------------- |
| Task Handling Time   | Baseline | Improved | 42% reduction        |
| CSAT Score           | Baseline | Improved | 64% increase         |
| Initial Containment  | 0%       | 40-45%   | Immediate impact     |
| Agent Daily Closures | Baseline | +31%     | With Copilot rollout |

### Qualitative Results

- Had been "trying to build declarative chatbots for a very long time"
- Limitations in flexibility and adaptability with declarative approach
- Needed solution that could evolve with growing needs
- "Just turning it on was like 40-45% containment"
- "Everyone was initially shocked and unsure how it could be that good"
- Billing inquiries constitute ~50% of all email inquiries
- AI handles inquiries without human intervention
- Allows team to provide personalized experience for complex interactions
- Enhanced collaboration between support and development teams
