### Quantitative Results

| ---------------------- | --------- | ---------------- | ----------------- |

### Qualitative Results

- Over 1,000 active AI use cases across the organization
- AI expected to direct more than half of all customer calls by end of 2025
- Focus on reducing administrative burdens
- Claims Assistant AI simplifies out-of-network claim submissions
- Cuts claim submission time from 12-15 minutes to 3 minutes
- Claims completion rates nearly doubled with AI assistance
- AI embedded throughout member-facing chatbots
- Leading insurer in AI deployment scale
