### Quantitative Results

| Metric                     | Before   | After        | Change              |
| -------------------------- | -------- | ------------ | ------------------- |
| AI Call Volume Target      | 0%       | 40%          | 40% target          |
| Case Escalations to Humans | 75%      | 35% (target) | -53% reduction      |

### Qualitative Results

- Goal to have Agentforce handle 40% of customer call volume

- AI agents handle routine inquiries while humans focus on complex issues
- Scalable approach to seasonal retail demand fluctuations
