## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric                  | Before   | After      | Change           |
| ----------------------- | -------- | ---------- | ---------------- |
| First Reply Time        | Baseline | Improved   | 64% faster       |
| Resolution Time         | Baseline | Improved   | 34% faster       |
| One-Touch Response Rate | N/A      | 80%        | Industry-leading |
| Phone Answer Rate       | Baseline | 95%        | 90% increase     |
| Monthly Tickets         | 10,000   | 10,000     | Scale maintained |
| CSAT                    | Baseline | +10 points | Significant lift |
| Email Automation        | 0%       | 64%        | Full automation  |

### Qualitative Results

- Unicorn fintech ($1B valuation) in just 4 years
- Recognized on TIME100 Next, LinkedIn Top Startups, Forbes Fintech50
- Previous stack: Intercom (help center), Front (ticketing), Aircall (phone)
- Consolidated all tools into Zendesk omnichannel platform
- Half of tickets via voice, half via email
- AI summarization helps onboard new reps faster
- "Zendesk AI simplifies and eases the workflow on our agent side"
- Saves time searching through entire conversation threads
