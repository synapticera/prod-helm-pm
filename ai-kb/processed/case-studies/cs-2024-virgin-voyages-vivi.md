### Qualitative Results

- First GenAI-powered chatbot built on Salesforce
- Answers complex, multi-part questions using knowledge base
- Maintains brand voice throughout conversations
- Continuous learning through agent interactions
- Elevated customer experience
- Alleviated management costs

---

## Implementation Details

- Vivi is one of the first generative AI-powered chatbots built on Salesforce
- Set new standard for online customer service
- As agents interact with customers, the framework crafts and refines Salesforce Knowledge articles
- Articles are reviewed and approved by Sailor Services team
- Continuous improvement ensures Vivi gets smarter over time
