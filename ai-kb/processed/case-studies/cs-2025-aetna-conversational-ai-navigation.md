## Results & Metrics

### Quantitative Results

| Metric             | Before   | After           | Change                 |
| ------------------ | -------- | --------------- | ---------------------- |
| Monthly App Users  | Baseline | ~50% of members | Half of active members |
| Digital Investment | N/A      | $20 billion     | Multi-year commitment  |
| Nurse Time Savings | Baseline | 90 min/day      | Full day/week saved    |

### Qualitative Results

- Paradigm shift from transactional to consumer health experience
- Industry-first dynamically generated rich UI (GenUI) components
- AI embedded throughout end-to-end digital experience, not just chat window
- Session awareness makes experience feel less like traditional chatbot
- No healthcare jargon required from members
- Provides maps, charts, financial breakdowns, informational cards tailored to questions
- Personalized cost breakdowns including estimates for fees and copayments
- Proactively provides related information (e.g., post-surgical rehab after surgery query)
- Voice enablement planned for first half of 2026
- AI-driven clinical care summaries save nurses 90 minutes per day
- Part of CVS Health's $20 billion digital investment
