### Qualitative Results

- HubSpot's Customer Support Team uses its own Service Hub
- $2.3M in annual headcount savings achieved
- Breeze customer agent provides 24/7 support
- 75% satisfaction rating with AI agent
- Many customers mistake AI agent for real person
- Helpful and friendly approach
- Especially valuable during off-hours
- Continuous support on weekends and after business hours
- Setup takes less than an hour
- Minimal upkeep since it taps directly into existing knowledge bases
- Setup described as "very straightforward"
- "A lot easier to maintain than a traditional chatbot"
- Frees up team to review content gaps and improve knowledge base
