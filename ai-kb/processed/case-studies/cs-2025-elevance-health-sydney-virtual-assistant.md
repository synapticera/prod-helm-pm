### Qualitative Results

- AI-driven virtual assistant reaches 22 million members
- ChatGPT-like conversational experience for benefits and coverage questions
- Nearly 90% of users found answers through virtual assistant or digital tools
- Uses 500 personalized data points including benefits, conditions, location, provider network
- Top queries: benefits questions, finding care, general insurance questions
- Supports Spanish language conversations
- AI-driven personalized matching for provider search
- Shows deductible spending details
- Can explain coverage for medical visits and procedures
- Provides cost estimates for care
