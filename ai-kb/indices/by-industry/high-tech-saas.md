## Industry-Specific Insights

### Key Trends (2025)

- **Platform Vendor Validation**: Tech companies proving their own AI (HubSpot Breeze, Freshworks Freddy, Zoom ZVA)
- **Resolution Rate Transformation**: Vagaro went from 4% to 44% (11x improvement)
- **Training Time Reduction**: Fisher & Paykel cut training from 9 months to 3 weeks (76% reduction)
- **Agentforce Momentum**: Multiple enterprise deployments showing 85%+ autonomous resolution

### Use Case Patterns

1. **Technical Support**: Complex product troubleshooting with knowledge base integration
2. **Onboarding Support**: New user activation and setup assistance
3. **Billing & Account**: Self-service for subscription management
4. **Product Questions**: Feature guidance and how-to assistance
5. **Bug Reporting**: Triage and escalation to engineering

### ROI Drivers

- High cost per human interaction ($25-$45/ticket)
- Technical complexity enabling AI knowledge base leverage
- 24/7 global support requirements
- Rapid scaling needs during product launches
- Developer self-service expectations
